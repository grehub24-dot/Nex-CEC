Chapter 3 Methodology
3.1 Chapter Introduction
Give a brief introduction to the chapter by indicating what the chapter entails.
3.2 Research Design/Approach
This part of the project should discuss the approaches the user intends to use, and
clearly stated reasons for choosing them. What is the plan of action (strategy) that
governs the choice and use of methods /approaches/procedures to investigate the
problem and to provide the solution? Think of how to elicit and analyse data (in the
form of requirements and test results), test your solution, write, etc? Why this
strategy? E.g. experimental, survey, action, case study, etc.
3.3 Research Setting
This part discusses, the cultural, physical or social setting in which the experiment
is conducted. May be more applicable in Lab setting and involves either controlled
or uncontrolled processes and environmental conditions. Where are you carrying out
the research? Describe key aspects of the place relevant to the research. E.g. HR sys.
for a particular organization; where is that organization, what do they do, what is
their HR structure, etc.
3.4 Data (Requirements) Collection Instrument
Appropriate data collection instrument must be employed. This includes interviews,
questionnaires, observation and document analysis for requirements gathering. This
may also include the use of computer tools to generate the appropriate data. Explain
the specific tool(s) (e.g. observation, questionnaires, interviews, etc.) you are using to
collect your data (requirements, test results)? NB: you will explain exactly how you
deployed this tool(s) under section 3.8.2.

3.5 Ethical Consideration
Where sensitive data is required, appropriate ethical consideration must be
employed. You must seek prior permission from your supervisor or appropriate
body mandated by the university. What did you do to protect the privacy,
confidentiality and even health of the research subjects, regarding the information
they provide, the testing they do, etc. The ethical clearance approval so received,
must be added to the appendices section of the report.
3.6 Description of Proposed System
Provide a brief but detailed description of the proposed system.
3.7 System Development/Building
3.7.1 Tools
Tell us about the tools you intend to use/used to develop your system and why the
chosen tools. For example, the programming language, database management
system, network simulation software, web development framework or library etc.
3.7.2 Development Process
Describe to us the particular system development process you intend to use to
develop your system and why. For a chosen development process, tell us exactly
what you would do/did at each phase. Read the notes under Section 3.8** as a guide.
3.8** The System Development Life Cycle(SDLC)
Describe what you have done under each phase of the SDLC: Planning, Analysis,
Design and Implementation. Computer network projects should make reference to
these key network development life cycles;
Analysis, Design, Simulation/Prototyping, Implementation, and Management
(ADSIM) or Prepare, Plan, Design, Implement, Operate, and Optimize (PPDIOO).
3.8.1 The Planning Phase
Describe how your team planned for the project with reference to project
initiation and project management.

3.8.2 Analysis Phase
Describe how requirements were gathered, and analyzed for the project. State

The project team by now should describe how the team designed/ will design

g. Site map and Wireframe

i. Network diagrams

Briefly explain how the team will develop the system. List the tools that would
be used to build and implement the system. Describe the tools and
technologies used for the system implementation based on the design phase,
and briefly explain why each tool or technology was chosen.
